## LIFESTYLE IMAGE PROMPTS

### Brand Guidelines for All Images

**Color Palette Reference:**
- Azure: #05436e (deep blue)
- Gold: #bc9833 (warm gold accents)
- Navy: #051c3b (dark backgrounds)
- Cream/warm whites for lifestyle settings

**Style Direction:**
- Editorial lifestyle photography
- Warm, natural lighting (golden hour preferred)
- Unhurried, confident, serene mood
- Pacific Northwest context where appropriate
- Premium but approachable
- NO clinical/hospital aesthetics
- NO stock photo awkwardness
- NO forced diversity casting

**Technical Specs:**
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 for heroes, 4:3 for cards, 1:1 for profiles
- Resolution: Minimum 2400px on longest edge
- Format: PNG or high-quality JPG

## GENERATION NOTES

**For Midjourney:**
- Add `--ar 16:9` or appropriate aspect ratio
- Add `--style raw` for more photorealistic results
- Add `--v 6` for latest version
- Add `--q 2` for higher quality

**For DALL-E 3:**
- Prompts work as written
- May need to request "photorealistic" explicitly
- Generate at highest resolution available

**For Ideogram/Others:**
- Add "photorealistic editorial photography" to beginning
- Specify "no text, no watermarks"
